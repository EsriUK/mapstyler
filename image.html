Mapstyler help - image

To get started, simply drag an image from another browser window or your computer and drop onto the map. The map will be re-styled using colours from the the dropped image.

Once you've dropped your image, click shuffle to change what elements of the map are assigned which colours from the image.

When you're happy, click the heart button in the top right to save a copy of this map to your ArcGIS Online account for use in your own maps and apps.

Built using Esri's dark gray canvas vector tile layer, version 4 of the ArcGIS API for JavaScript, Color Thief and RainbowVis-JS.
